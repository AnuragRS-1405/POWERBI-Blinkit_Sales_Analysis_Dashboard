# Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard


An interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze Blinkit's grocery sales data and provide actionable business insights. This project leverages **Power BI** for visualization, **Power Query** for data transformation, and **DAX** for creating dynamic measures and KPIs.


## Project Overview

The Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard helps visualize and monitor key business metrics such as total sales, average sales, customer ratings, outlet performance, and product category trends. The dashboard enables users to explore sales data through interactive filters and provides meaningful insights into outlet performance, product performance, customer preferences, and overall business growth.


## Project Objectives

1. Analyze overall sales performance across Blinkit outlets.
2. Track important KPIs including Total Sales, Average Sales, Number of Items, Number of Outlets, and Average Rating.
3. Compare outlet performance based on outlet type, outlet size, and location.
4. Identify top-performing product categories.
5. Analyze the relationship between product visibility and sales.
6. Enable interactive exploration of data using slicers and filters.

## Dashboard Pages

### Overview Dashboard
The Overview page provides a high-level summary of Blinkit's overall business performance.
Key Highlights: Total Sales, Average Sales, Number of Items, Number of Outlets, Average Rating, Sales Trend by Outlet Establishment Year, Sales by Outlet Size and type, Top 5 Best-Selling Categories and Sales Distribution by Fat Content

### Outlet Analysis
The Outlet Analysis page focuses on outlet performance and customer trends.
Key Highlights: Revenue Distribution by Outlet Size, Revenue by Outlet Location, Revenue by Outlet Type, Average Customer Ratings, Outlet Performance Matrix and Comparison of Sales across Outlet Types

### Product Analysis
The Product Analysis page provides detailed insights into product performance.
Key Highlights: Top Selling Product Categories, Product Performance Matrix, Item Visibility vs Sales Analysis, Sales Distribution by Fat Content and Average Sales by Product Category

## Business Insights

Some key insights obtained from the dashboard include:
1. Supermarket Type1 contributes the highest revenue among all outlet types.
2. Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are the best-selling product categories.
3. Low Fat products generate a larger share of overall sales compared to Regular products.
4. Medium-sized outlets contribute the highest sales.
5. Sales peaked around the 2018 outlet establishment period.
6. Item visibility has varying influence on product sales across categories.
7. Tier 3 locations generate the highest revenue compared to other outlet locations.
